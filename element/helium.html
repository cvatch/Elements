Helium

History Uses Objects Locations Characters

History

Discovery: Helium was first discovered in the Sun by George Johnstone Stoney in 1868, during a solar eclipse. It was later isolated on Earth by William Gilbert Thornton in 1895.

Helium’s presence in stars was crucial in understanding stellar nuclear fusion, as it is a byproduct of the fusion process that powers stars.

Uses

Helium is used in a variety of ways in both the real world and the world of The Elemental Chronicles:

  • Helium is used to fill balloons and airships, as it is lighter than air and non-flammable, making it safer than hydrogen.
  • It is used in cryogenics for cooling superconducting magnets, such as in MRI machines.
  • Helium has a crucial role in the scientific field, specifically in particle accelerators and in the study of quantum mechanics.

Objects

In The Elemental Chronicles, Helium is linked to several important artifacts:

  • Helium Heart: Created by Helion, the Star Lord of Helium, this artifact channels the essence of helium to stabilize cosmic energy and promote harmony in the universe.
  • Helium Crystal: A mysterious artifact that harnesses the power of helium gas to manipulate the physical properties of matter and energy.

Locations

Helium is associated with several notable locations in The Elemental Chronicles:

  • Helium Nexus: A cosmic center where Helion, the Star Lord of Helium, forged many of his greatest creations, including the Helium Heart.
  • The Celestial Forge: A sacred place where elemental forces, including helium, are used in the creation of powerful artifacts that shape the fate of the cosmos.

Characters

The following characters are closely linked to Helium in The Elemental Chronicles:

  • Helion: The Star Lord of Helium, Helion is a cosmic being who played a significant role in the stabilization of early cosmic forces and the creation of several key artifacts.
  • Aurora: A celestial figure tied to the Helium Nexus, known for harnessing helium’s power to manipulate light and energy.
